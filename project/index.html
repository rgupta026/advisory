ABOUT CASTON CORPORATE ADVISORY

We are a corporate advisory firm with interests in Investment Banking, and transaction advisory services. Caston Corporate advisory was set up in 2007 and has rapidly built significant competitive presence in the North India.

The team at Caston Corporate Advisory has shown up very transparent and unique working processes to its clients, and has been able to build continuing relationship with them.

With various strategic cross border as well as domestic tie- ups across one of the best financial houses, we are able to deliver best in class fund raising and M&A services to our corporate clients. Also, efforts are being made to internally equip ourselves to strengthen our brand as well as service delivery.

At Caston Corporate Advisory, we envision to become one of the finest investment Banks in India and also support our customers with direct lending for their strategic commercial needs. Our forte is in Asset Management, Credit analysis, fund arrangement and risk advisory.

Caston Corporate Advisory has been consistently achieving various milestones and is poised to become a well known figure in the Indian Capital market.
